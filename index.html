About Me

Hi! My name is Mai (pronounced like "my") and I am a Spanish expat living in Atlanta, GA, for the last 5 years. I have worked in several different industries, including hospitality, non-profit and as tech support. I've been dabbing into WordPress for a few years now, creating blogs and customizing them. In 2018, I developed my first WordPress website for my sister's acting school. It involved a lot of Googling and a few headaches, but I was very proud of the outcome. While revamping her website in 2019, I decided I wanted to pursue more formal education and I signed up for the Break Into Tech course from Skillcrush. The rest is history!

I am an avid learner looking for new challenges. I can't wait to work with you!
